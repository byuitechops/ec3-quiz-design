DESCRIBE SURROUNDINGS

GOAL: Describe immediate surroundings

Learn

When describing immediate surroundings, tell about what you see. Tell about location and how the place looks. Tell about the size and color of the buildings or trees and plants.

When describing the inside of your house or apartment, tell about what you see. Tell about the location of appliances and furniture. Tell about the size of the rooms.

Vocabulary

Surroundings: the things around you

Location: the place of something

Study

Describe a place

The church is near the grocery store on a busy street. It is white and made of bricks. It has two doors in the front and a beautiful steeple. It is surrounded by trees.

Describe a home

My apartment has two bedrooms, a kitchen, a living room, and one bathroom. It has brown carpet. The fridge is in the kitchen next to the stove. One bedroom is large. One is small. I have a small blue sofa by the window.

Practice

Describe your home. Where is it located? What is the size? What is near your home?

Describe the inside of your home. How many bedrooms does it have? What rooms does it have? How big are the rooms? What kinds of furniture do you have? What appliances do you have?
