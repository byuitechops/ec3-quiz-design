OPINIONS

GOAL: Learn how to give opinions and ask for opinions.

Learn

When we talk to people, we often give an opinion or ask for their opinions. It is good to find out what other people think. The phrases below are used in conversations.


Review the phrases below.

Giving an opinion

  • In my opinion...
  • If you want my honest opinion...
  • According to...
  • As far as I'm concerened...
  • If you ask me...

Asking for an opinion

  • What do you think?
  • What are your thoughts?
  • How do you feel about that?
  • Do you have anything to say?
  • Do you agree?
