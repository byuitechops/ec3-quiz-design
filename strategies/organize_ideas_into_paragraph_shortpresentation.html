Learn to organize ideas

CAN-DO: Can organize ideas into an appropriate structure/outline

Learn

Before you write or talk about a topic, it is a good idea to first think of all the ideas you can about the topic. Then you can choose the best ideas from your list and organize the ideas in the order you want to present them.

Study

Topic - Learning to swim


Step 1 - List all the ideas that you can think of on the topic.

Lessons

Swimming pool

Ocean

River

Teacher

Patience

Fear

Safety

Flotation tools

Practice

Goals

Determination

Step 2 - Decide which ideas you want to write or talk about and what order to put them in. Develop categories and add details. You now have an outline that you can follow when you write or talk about the topic.

  1. Lessons & teacher - best way to learn
  2. Types of water - pool, ocean, river; pool is the easiest and safest
  3. Challenges & solutions - overcoming fear; use flotation devices
  4. Strategies - set goals, practice, be determined & have fun

Practice

Review the example below of how to use the ideas in your list to write a paragraph

The best way to learn to swim is to take lessons from a teacher. You can learn to swim in different types of water, such as a pool, the ocean, or a river. A oool is the easiest and safest. When you learn to swim, you need to overcome challenges. You might be afraid of the water. A solution is to use a flotation device. Strategies for learning to swim are to set goals, practice, and be determined. Don't forget to have fun!

Do

Write a paragraph or tell someone about learning how to do something like riding a bike, painting a picture, or playing the piano. Decide how to order your ideas. What should come first? What should come second? What will you save until the end? Share your ideas with a friend or family member.
