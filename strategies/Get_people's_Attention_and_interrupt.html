Get people's attention and interrupt

- CAN-DO: Can ask and respond to requests and offers

Learn

You many need to stop someone and ask that person a question. You can interrupt or stop a conversation politely. You can stop someone and ask for information. You can also raise your hand and ask a teacher a question or answer a question.


Try and make eye contact with the person first. Sometimes you can signal a person with a wave. In a classroom, you can put up your hand and wait to be called on.

Vocabulary

  • Polite: respectful
  • Attention: to notice
  • Interrupt: stop someone from doing something

Study

Get Someone’s Attention in a Restaurant or Store

  • Excuse me.
  • Pardon me.

Get Attention and Interrupt in a Class, a Meeting, at Work, or a Conversation

  • I’m sorry to interrupt, but
  • I’d like to say something here.
  • Excuse me.
  • May I comment on that?

Get attention and Interrupt on the Street

  • Excuse me.
  • Do you have a minute?

Responses

  • Yes, what can I do for you?
  • Sure
  • No problem.

Practice

Readthe conversation.Identify the interruption. Identify the situation.

  • Secretary: May I interrupt you for a moment?
  • Boss: Yes. What can I do for you?
  • Secretary: I just need your signature on this document.
  • Boss: Okay. Here you go.
  • Secretary: Thanks. Sorry for the interruption.
  • Boss: No problem.
