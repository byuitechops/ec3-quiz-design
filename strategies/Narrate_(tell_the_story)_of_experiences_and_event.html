Narrate Experiences/Factual Events

GOAL: Narrate (tell the story) of experiences and events

Learn

When we tell stories of true things that happened, we are narrating. When we narrate, we use sequence words and past tense verbs.

Narration:

Sequence word, + Subject + Verb-ed + Object

First, I walked to school.

Second, I studied English.

Vocabulary

SEQUENCE SIGNAL WORDS
 Beginning

First, At first, In the beginning, To begin with,initially,In the first stage/phase

Before that, earlier, Previously, Prior to, From this point
Middle
Afterwarsd, After that, Later, Some time later, Following that, Soon, Soon after, then, Meanwhile, During that time, The next step is/was to, Subsequently, Consequently
End
Finally, Lastly, At last, In the end, To conclude, In conclusion

Study

One day, I was hungry.

First, I ate ice cream

After that, I ate cake.

Then I ate lots of candy

During that time, I felt sick.

In the end, I stopped eating.
