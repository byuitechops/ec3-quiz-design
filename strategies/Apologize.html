MAKE APPOINTMENTS

GOAL: Apologize

Learn

You can apologize to someone when you do something that makes someone unhappy or if you make a mistake. Situations that need an apology include:

  • Bumping into a stranger
  • Showing up late for an appointment
  • Forgetting something important

Vocabulary

  • Apolize: to feel sorry and ask forgiveness
  • Remedy: a fix
  • Fault: to be responsible
  • Assurance: a promise

Study

Vocabulary

  • Please forgive me.
  • I apologize for _____.
  • I’m very sorry

Explain/Give excuse

  • I wasn’t thinking.
  • I didn’t realize that ____.
  • It was an accident.

Offer to remedy

  • Let me help you.
  • How can I make it up to you?

Response

  • That’s okay
  • You don’t need to apologize.
  • Don’t worry about it.

Give assurance

  • It’s entirely my fault

Practice

Readthe conversation. How does Abigail offer a remedy?

  • Abigail:I’m so sorry that I hurt your feelings. Please forgive me. I didn’t mean to hurt you.
  • Jill: It’s not your fault. You didn’t know.
  • Abigail: I feel terrible about it. How can I make it up to you?
  • Jill: Don’t worry about it. It’s okay. Let’s just forget about it.

Writea text to a friend. Apologize for forgetting her birthday. Give an excuse. Offer a remedy.

Speakto a partner. Respond to this apology

  • I apologize for being late. I didn't realize that it was so late. I just lost truck of time. Sorry.

Answers

Read

  • Abigail: I’m so sorry that I hurt your feelings. Please forgive me. I didn’t mean to hurt you.
  • Jill: It’s not your fault. You didn’t know
  • Abigail: I feel terrible about it. How can I make it up to you?
  • Jill: Don’t worry about it. It’s okay. Let’s just forget about it.

Write

  • Example: Abigail, I am so sorry I forgot your birthday! I didn’t realize it was yesterday. Let me make it up to you!

Speak

  • Example: Don't worry about it. I understand.
